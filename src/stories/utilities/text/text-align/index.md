# Text Alignment

### Usage
```html
<p class="dp-text-align-left">
  The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
</p>
<p class="dp-text-align-center">
  The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
</p>
<p class="dp-text-align-right">
  The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
</p>
```
